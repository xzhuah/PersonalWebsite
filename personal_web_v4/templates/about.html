About me

A Naive Happy Guy

My parents named me "Xinyu"(昕宇) which means the rising sun in the universe in Chinese. My parents never received education as good as me, but I have to agree that they left me a good name. No matter where I am and no matter what situation I am faced, I always believe that I can be the rising sun, spreading hope to the world continuously and gathering power unstoppably. I also love the quality of the universe. Its selfless toleration has the ability to accommodate the world. Stay naive and stay young, listen to everyone and learn from people around, that is why the universe can be the universe.

Life can be more dramatic than drama because we always have enough time to make a change. I can always remember a guy who fight me a lot when I was a first year primary school student became my "errand" boy 5 years later. Things that past and people you have met, all of these are the witnesses of your growth. I am happy that I am always naive because that gives me endless chances to grow up and make sincere friends. As my favorite anime character Naruto said, I want to become stronger to protect more people. There are too many beautiful places and things in the world and that makes all the costs deserved.
